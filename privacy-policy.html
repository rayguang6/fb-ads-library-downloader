Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Facebook Ads Library Downloader Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that you understand how your data is handled.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects and processes the following types of information:

  • Authentication information when you sign in using your email
  • Session tokens for maintaining your login state
  • Content you choose to download from the Facebook Ads Library
  • Technical information necessary for the extension to function properly

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • Authenticating your access to the service
  • Storing and organizing your downloaded content
  • Maintaining and improving the extension's functionality
  • Ensuring secure access to your saved data

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored in a Supabase database. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information and data you collect.

Browser Permissions

Our extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your preferences and authentication information
  • ActiveTab: To interact with Facebook Ads Library pages
  • Scripting: To extract content from the ads library
  • Host permissions: To connect with Facebook and our storage service

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is only used within the extension and stored in your personal database space.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of data collection by uninstalling the extension

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure data transmission using HTTPS
  • Authentication tokens for secure access
  • Database encryption
  • Regular security updates

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
